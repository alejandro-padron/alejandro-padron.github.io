Nissan Skyline R32 GTR

The Nissan Skyline R32 GTR is one of the most well known and sought after cars in the car world. It's also known as the Godzilla because of how dominant is was in it's debut. It was powered by a 2.6L RB26 engine that produced 276 horsepower. Even though it wasn't really the lightest car around, the modablity of the engine was immense which made it really popular.

Unfortunately, since the Skyline R32 is really popular, the price is really high at around $40,000
