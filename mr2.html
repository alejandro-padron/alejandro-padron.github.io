Toyota MR2 AW11

The Toyota MR2 AW11 is one of the best cars that I have ever come across. It's small but has more room that what the ourside suggest. Since it's really light, you don't really need that much power for it to be quick. One of the best things about it is that it has one of the legendary engine, which is the 4AGE. Not only is that engine really good for modding, it's also a mid-engine car, which means that the cornering feels a lot better and more responsive.

Luckily, the MR2 is pretty affordable with a price of around $5,000.
I personally thing that for the price, it's a really good car.
