Mazda RX7 FD

The Mazda RX7 FD is one if not the best car that Mazda has produced. The engine is one of a kind combustion engine that is wildly different from the rest of the engines. Most people have a love-hate relationship with the rotary engines since they break down easily, but they sound really nice and produce a decent amount of horsepower.

Unfortunately, since the rx7 is one of the well known cars in the car community, its also pretty expensive. It costs around $35,000 to get your hands on one.
